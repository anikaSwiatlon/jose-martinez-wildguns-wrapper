Free, open source, MIT-licensed. Manifest V3 browser extension — Chrome, Edge, Brave, Opera, Firefox (temporary add-on).

What it does

📜 Save battle reports in one click

Open a fight report, click Save report in the popup, and I file away both the structured details and the full report DOM. Metadata goes to your Supabase, the heavy stuff into a shared Astra DB. No more downloading JSON files just to chuck them in a folder.

📦 Send resources by coordinates

Type the coords, hit send. No more squinting at the market screen while half-asleep. Save your daily transports as named presets so next time it's one click. Up to ten presets, oldest one quietly makes way for the newest.

🤝 Plays nice with your tribe

Paste a TSV/CSV from a spreadsheet straight into the transports table — one row per ally, four resource columns. Up to four requests fire in parallel, with a status mark on every row so you know what landed.

🛡️ Polite about your data

No third-party tracking, no analytics, no telemetry. Your Supabase credentials stay in your browser. The only data that ever leaves your machine is the report you explicitly save, going to the databases you've configured. More on this →
